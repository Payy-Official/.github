# payy

**Payy** is a modern, privacy-focused e-commerce platform built for selling digital products.

It provides creators, developers, and online businesses with the tools needed to launch and manage digital storefronts — without unnecessary data collection or complexity.

## What is Payy?

Payy acts as a full e-commerce portal where sellers can:
- Create and manage digital product listings
- Accept online payments through supported payment gateways
- Deliver digital goods automatically
- Manage customers, orders, and payouts from a single dashboard

The platform is designed to be lightweight, fast, and scalable, making it suitable for both small creators and larger marketplaces.

## Core Principles

- **Privacy-first** – minimal data collection, no invasive tracking
- **Simplicity** – clean UI and straightforward workflows
- **Automation** – instant delivery and order handling
- **Scalability** – built to grow with your business

## Status

Payy is currently under active development.  
Some features may be experimental or unavailable as the platform evolves.
